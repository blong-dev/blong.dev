Port Foliopolis — playable LLM-NPC portfolio
Author

The front door of blong.dev rendered as a Phaser retro RPG where the NPCs are live LLMs (Gemini and Groq behind a Cloudflare Pages Function).

A fail-closed cost guard makes a public LLM toy that structurally cannot run up a bill: per-IP and global KV-backed daily caps, free-tier provider keys with no billing attached, a kill switch that fails closed, optional Cloudflare AI Gateway routing, and counters that bump only on success. Built in roughly a weekend; it's a sample of the velocity, not the headline.
