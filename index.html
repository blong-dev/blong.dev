About

I'm Braedon Long — an enterprise architect who came up through data: analytics, engineering, architecture. For years I made the software and vendor calls for mid-to-large companies. Now I build my own.

The work above is what I'm shipping; Port Foliopolis up top is that same stack, having fun. What I care about most is lifting the weight of technology off small businesses — local heroes getting squeezed by giants who can afford armies of engineers. I want to be that for the people who can't.
